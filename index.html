Biography

I am currently a Master Candidate in the Intelligent Computing Research Centre at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology (shenzhen) under the supervision of Professor Ruifeng Xu.

I obtained my Bachelor degree in Software School, Xiamen University under the supervision of Professor Jinsong Su in June, 2018. My Bachelor Thesis focus on Image Caption Generation.

Research Interests

My research interests focus on Natural Language Processing, especially on sentiment analysis and dialogue system.
